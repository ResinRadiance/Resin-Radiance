About Us

Resin Radiance is a self-made company founded by Sweta Srivastava. We specialize in creating eco-friendly resin products like keychains, bookmarks, coasters, and decorative items for festive and religious occasions. Each item is uniquely handcrafted to reflect a blend of sustainability and art.
